The provided statement makes use of the Data Definition Language (DML) 
component of SQL. The "DROP DATABASE" statement removes, deletes a databse
entirely from a user. In other words, it manipulates a database's structure 
(or rather deletes it entirely). As such, 'DROP DATABASE' is considered a 
DML statement.
