The provided statement makes use of the Data Manipulation Language (DML) 
component of SQL. The "DELETE FROM" statement deletes, removes data from a 
table. In other words, it manipulates data itself, rather than defining its 
structure. As such, 'DELETE FROM' is considered a DML statement.
