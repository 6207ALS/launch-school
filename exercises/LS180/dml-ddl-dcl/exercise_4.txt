The provided statement makes use of the Data Definition Language (DDL)
component of SQL. The 'ALTER TABLE' and 'DROP CONSTRAINT' statements alter the
'structure', or schema, of a table; they manipulate the 'data definitions.' As
such, the statements are considered Data Definition Language.
