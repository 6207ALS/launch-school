A:
The provided statement makes use of the Data Manipulation Language (DML) 
component of SQL. The "INSERT INTO" statement adds data, record(s), to a table.
In other words, it manipulates data itself, rather than defining its structure.
As such, 'INSERT INTO' is considered a DML statement.
